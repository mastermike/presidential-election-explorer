Method note

Candidate lines are aggregated within each state, including fusion party lines. “Other” includes candidate lines outside the Democratic and Republican nominees; administrative blank, void, overvote, and undervote rows are excluded from popular-vote shares. Electoral-vote values show each jurisdiction’s allocation for that election, not district-level awards in Maine or Nebraska.
